Your Thoughts Matter

Promoting growth mindset in girls and boys
Your child’s mindset matters, more than they realize. Help them understand the power of a growth mindset. Told in an engaging way that brings clarity to the subject of mindset, ‘Your Thoughts Matter’ gives concrete examples of what different mindsets sound like in our heads. by Esther Pia Cordova, author
