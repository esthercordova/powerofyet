About the author

When I was about 7 years old I had a phase during which I would’t do any of my homework alone because I thought I couldn’t do it. And sometimes now I have this voice in my head that tells me that I can’t do something.

I recently went to a coding bootcamp. This experience was the hardest and most challenging thing I’ve ever done. And that’s where I learned about the growth mindset and the powerful word YET. It’s such a simple concept but important concept.

I would like for every child and their parents to embrace the growth mindset. I’m not a fan of telling our kids that they can become anything. I think it’s only fair to tell them, they can become nearly anything, if they work hard and put a lot of effort into what they do.

Esther
